About Bioremax Innovation Private Limited

Bioremax Innovation Private Limited (BIPL) is a research startup company registered under I-Startup Rajasthan and MSE with the Rajasthan Government. Founded on 18th July 2024, BIPL specializes in developing innovative, environmentally sustainable solutions. Based in Jaipur, Rajasthan, the company is driven by a team of over six scientists and employees.

BIPL has developed an enzyme-based bioremediation process capable of breaking down all fractions of oily sludge, crude sludge, and drill cuttings. This process is 100% natural, eco-friendly, and cost-effective. It transforms harmful hydrocarbons into non-toxic fatty acids through enzymes sourced from naturally occurring microbes.

As the first startup recognized by the Rajasthan government for science-based pollutant site restoration, BIPL leads the way in biotechnological innovation. Its proprietary super enzyme offers a pioneering solution for the safe treatment of oil-based environmental pollutants.
