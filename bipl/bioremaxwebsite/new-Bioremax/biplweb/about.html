About Bioremax Innovation Pvt. Ltd.

Driving sustainable biotechnology innovations that transform industries and protect our environment.

Bioremax Innovation Private Limited (BIPL) is a registered startup under the Startup India initiative and classified as a Micro and Small Enterprise. Established on 18th July 2024, BIPL is dedicated to developing environmentally sustainable green technologies, driven by a team of passionate scientists.
Our core innovation lies in a breakthrough enzyme-based bioremediation process that enables the natural decomposition of hydrocarbon compounds found in oily sludge, crude oil-contaminated soil, oil spill sites, oily waste materials, and oil-based drill cuttings.

This advanced bioremediation technology is 100% eco-friendly and cost-effective, transforming harmful hydrocarbons into safe fatty acids and water, thereby significantly reducing environmental impact while promoting sustainable waste management solutions.
Our proprietary BIOREMAX ECODIGESTASE™ enzyme-based bioremediation technology ensures the safe and effective treatment of toxic hydrocarbon compounds in contaminated soil and water across operational areas of Oil Producing and Oil Marketing Companies. The solution is designed to support a circular, zero-waste approach, enabling the transformation of hazardous waste into environmentally safe byproducts.

Driven by a vision of a cleaner and more sustainable planet, Bioremax Innovation Private Limited (BIPL) continues to deliver science-driven, eco-friendly solutions that benefit both industrial operations and natural ecosystems.
