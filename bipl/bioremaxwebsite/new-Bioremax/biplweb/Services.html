Our Services

At Bioremax Innovation Pvt. Ltd (BIPL), we combine biotechnology and environmental science to create sustainable industrial solutions that protect our earth.

Enzyme-Based Bioremediation of Total Petroleum Hydrocarbons/ Oily sludge

Our proprietary enzyme based formulations efficiently degrade Total Petroleum Hydrocarbons (TPH) /crude oil, Oily Sludge, oily based Chemical sludge/ ETP Sludge, oil based drill cuttings and organic waste into fatty acids and water as byproducts, restoring environmental balance.

Learn More
Environmental Restoration

We provide In Situ and Ex Situ Bioremediation in operation area of oil producing and oil marketing companies. We use enzyme-based formulations to restore TPH/hydrocarbon contaminated sites in operation area of oil producing and oil marketing companies, Petro Chemical Plants, Gas Cracker and Polymer Plants, Petroleum Oil Depots. Our enzyme based formulations are effective for Industrial Organic Waste Management We help industries to minimize waste generation and adopt enzyme based treatment technologies that are efficient and environmentally sound.
